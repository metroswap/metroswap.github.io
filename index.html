A Blockchain Indepedent Protocol for Automated Market Making on EVM Compatible Networks

A Blockchain Independent Protocol for Automated Market Making on EVM Compatible Blockchains. Guaranteed liquidity for millions of users and thousands of applications. The Metroswap protocol empowers developers, liquidity providers and traders to participate in a financial marketplace that is open and accessible to all.
